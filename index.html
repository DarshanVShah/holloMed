Our mission

Hollo Medical was founded to reduce the burden of chronic respiratory diseases.

How we do it

We have transformed current holding chambers into a device that can deliver more medication while fitting comfortably in your pocket!

Why we do it

Chambers are a necessity to make sure inhalers deliver the medication to the lungs, but current designs are bulky, embarrassing and unintuitive!
